Hi there!👋

I am currently a CS junior student in [Tongji University](https://en.tongji.edu.cn/p/). During my first three years of university, I majored in [Intelligent Construction](https://civileng.tongji.edu.cn/civilengen/18034/list.htm), in the [College of Civil Engineering](https://civileng.tongji.edu.cn/civilengen/main.htm).     
Driven by research interests, I transferred to Computer Science and will receive my Bachelor's degree in 2026 and actively seek 26fall PhD opportunities!🎓


Research Interests
======
My research interests currently lie in **open-set understanding for 3D scenes** and **AIGC for 3D vision**. In the future, I aim to explore the application of generative models in **3D reconstruction** and **3D scene re-editing**.   
Through developing scalable and efficient methods, I strive to bridge the gap between real-world data and AIGC, pushing the boundaries of 3D content generation and scene understanding for both academic and industrial applications.
